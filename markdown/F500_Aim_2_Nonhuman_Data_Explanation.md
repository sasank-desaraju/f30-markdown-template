## Aim 2 Data Not Considered Human Subjects Research {.unnumbered}

Aim 2 uses de-identified patient-derived glioblastoma cultures obtained from an existing biorepository after standard-of-care surgery.
The research team will not interact with patients, will not intervene in clinical care, and will not receive direct identifiers.
For a real application, this determination must be confirmed with the institution's IRB and described using institution-specific language.
