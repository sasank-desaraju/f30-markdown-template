# F30 Grant Tips

- Make aims independent enough that one disappointing result does not collapse the whole application.
- Explain why the candidate needs the training plan, not just why the project is interesting.
- Keep reviewer workload low: clear topic sentences, explicit alternatives, and visible milestones.
- Use Word exports for human markup, then reconcile changes back into Markdown.
- Keep feedback in one folder so agents and humans can reconstruct the history of revisions.
